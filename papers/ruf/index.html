When Restricted-Resources Decrease Consumption

Quentin André, Nicholas S. Reinholtz, John G. Lynch Jr.

1. Manifest

The Process Macro by Andrew F. Hayes has helped thousands of researchers in their analysis of moderation, mediation, and conditional processes. Unfortunately, Process was only available for proprietary softwares (SAS and SPSS), which means that students and researchers had to purchase a license of those softwares to make use of the Macro.

Because of the growing popularity of Python in the scientific community, I decided to implement the features of the Process Macro into an open-source library, that researchers will be able to use without relying on proprietary softwares. PyProcess
